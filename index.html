digital archive

welcome to the website of the digital archive.

https://digital-archive.github.io/ / http://digital-archive.tk/

this project aims to solve a problem common to some peoples everyday live its called (the evil) dead linkshave you ever had a project that you was trying to get on with and then suddenly you got stopped by some association without the sense of keeping important services up and running

have you ever wished that you had saved some pieces of information before, and then after that you had to realised that the piece you need is not anymore accessible

our project team got following intentions with this project

  • getting people sensible about the importance of keeping services up and running
  • helping people to find lost pieces of information
    • maybe someone got a backup to share...
  • creating appropriated software to archive important pices of information for later generations

if this textes could wake the understanding of the importance of this goals in you, or you would like to contact or help the team in any other way feel free to fill in an issue at

  • https://github.com/the-digital-archive/main-project-repository/issues

thanks for reading so far, we all hope that we will be able to solve much of the problems mentioned in the future
